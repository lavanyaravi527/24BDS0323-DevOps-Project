About the symposium

From one lecture hall to the whole campus.

TechNova is organised entirely by students, for students, with faculty acting as mentors rather than managers.

Our story

How TechNova started

TechNova began in 2023 when a group of final-year students organised a single-day paper-presentation event as their capstone project. What was meant to be a one-off event outgrew the lecture hall it started in within its first year.

By its second edition, the event had added a hackathon and a robotics track. This year's edition — the fourth — runs across three full days and six parallel tracks, with students from more than thirty partner colleges expected to attend.

Our mission

What we're trying to build

TechNova exists to give students a low-stakes, high-energy place to test ideas outside the classroom — to build something in 24 hours, present research to a real panel, or simply meet people working on the same problems at other colleges.

Every rupee raised beyond costs goes back into the department's student project lab for the following year.
