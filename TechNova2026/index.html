What it is

Built by students, for students who build things.

TechNova started in 2023 as a single-day paper-presentation event in a lecture hall. Four editions later, it's a three-day symposium spanning hackathons, robotics, workshops, and keynotes from engineers working across the industry.

Tracks

Pick your track, then pick your event.

Five tracks running in parallel across the three days — technical, creative, and everything in between.

Track 01

Hackathon

24-hour build sprint. Open problem statements across web, mobile, and embedded systems.

Track 02

Robotics & IoT

Line-following bots, maze solvers, and a sensor-fusion challenge judged live on the arena floor.

Track 03

Paper Presentation

Undergraduate and postgraduate research tracks, reviewed by a faculty and industry panel.

Track 04

Workshops

Half-day hands-on sessions on cloud infrastructure, ML systems, and secure coding.

Track 05

Case Study Challenge

Teams solve a real industry-sourced business-technology problem against the clock.

Track 06

Gaming & Esports

A lighter track to close each evening — LAN tournaments and a retro arcade corner.
