Events & tracks

Six tracks. Eighteen events. Pick more than one.

Every track runs independently, so you can move between a workshop in the morning and the hackathon floor by evening.

Track 01 · Build

Hackathon

A 24-hour build sprint. Teams of 2–4 pick from open problem statements across web, mobile, and embedded systems, and ship a working prototype by demo time.

Team size: 2–4 · Duration: 24 hrs · Prize pool: ₹80,000

Track 02 · Build

Robotics & IoT Challenge

Line-following, maze-solving, and a sensor-fusion obstacle round judged live on the arena floor. Bring your own bot or build on-site with kits provided.

Team size: 1–3 · Duration: 2 days · Prize pool: ₹60,000

Track 03 · Research

Paper Presentation

Undergraduate and postgraduate tracks reviewed by a joint faculty and industry panel. Submissions open four weeks before the event.

Format: Individual or pairs · Duration: 15 min slots · Prize pool: ₹40,000

Track 04 · Learn

Workshops

Half-day, hands-on sessions on cloud infrastructure, ML systems in production, and secure coding practices — run by working engineers.

Format: Individual · Duration: 3–4 hrs each · Certificate included

Track 05 · Solve

Case Study Challenge

Teams receive a real, anonymised industry problem statement and present a technology-backed solution to a panel of sponsors.

Team size: 3–4 · Duration: 6 hrs · Prize pool: ₹50,000

Track 06 · Unwind

Gaming & Esports

Evening LAN tournaments across two titles, plus a retro arcade corner running throughout the symposium.

Format: Solo & squads · Duration: Evenings, all 3 days · Prize pool: ₹20,000
